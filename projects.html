Research Projects

Motion planning and control in autonomous vehicles
performing drifting and evasive maneuvers

I developed an RC car and a simulation model with a motion planning algorithm
to control the car for steady-state drift cornering and evasive maneuvers.

Modelling the physical palpation examination
for diagnosis of abdominal adhesion

I designed an experimental procedure to mimic the physical palpation examination
and studied the rheological properties of skin to work on developing
a non-invasive method of diagnosing adhesion.

Waste heat driven adsorption cooling and desalination system

I conducted experiments on adsorption chillers to study the steady state thermal behavior
and measure their performance under different operating parameters.
